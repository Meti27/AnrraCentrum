Project: Railway Corridor VIII, Rehabilitation of Section Kumanovo-Belakovce.
Contracting Authority: Public enterprise Railways of the Republic of North Macedonia
Project Description:
The railway section from Kumanovo to Beljakovc is 30.8 km long, being the first of three general sections of railway tracks along the VIII International Railway Corridor, which should connect the Republic of Macedonia with the Republic of Bulgaria, starting from Kumanovo, passing through Beljakovce, Kriva Palanke to Deve Bair, i.e. to the border with the Republic of Bulgaria.
