About us

ANRRA CENTRUM LTD, was established as a construction company in Tetovo North Macedonia in 2005, it is a family company founded by the Alija family.
The company specializes in the construction and maintenance of civil works and road infrastructure, especially in the railway.

The “ANRRA CENTRUM LTD ” company is engaged in the construction and reconstruction of public objects, maintenance of civil works and road infrastructure and funded by the North Macedonian government and the private construction sector. We have also participated in projects financed by international institutions in the country and the region. ANRRA CENTRUM ltd has invested heavily in construction machinery, transport vehicles, and construction material manufacturing facilities.
