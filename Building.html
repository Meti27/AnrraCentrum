Finished Projects

Project: Reconstruction of the railway station in Tetovo.
Contracting Authority: Public Enterprise Railways of the Republic of North Macedonia
Project Description:
The railway station is located at the entrance to Tetovo.
The project included the complete reconstruction of the building, the new facade, the construction of a new sanitary environment, the new roof, asphalting of the parking lot, the replacement of doors and windows, a new electrical system, and the construction of sidewalks and the park.

Project: Construction of bridges in Rruga e Arberit ALBANIA
Contracting Authority: Ministry of Infrastructure and Energy
Project Description:
Construction of some parts of bridges on the international highway Rruga e Arberit in ALBANIA.

Project: Rehabilitation of the bed abd bridge of MRZENSKA river.
Contracting Authority: Public Enterprise Railways of the Republic of North Macedonia
Project Description:
The Mrzenska Bridge is located over the Mrzenska River in Eastern Macedonia and serves for the passage of the train.
This project included the reconstruction of the bridge and river bed, placing stone gabions on the river bed, and building a protective wall.
